Celebrate Faith, Family, and Legacy

Join us in beautiful Kirtland, Ohio, for an incredible weekend as we gather once again to celebrate faith, family, and the remarkable legacy of Kirtland, Ohio — The city of faith and beauty!

For generations, Kirtland has been a place of revelation, restoration, and gathering. Kirtland Heritage Days continues that tradition by bringing people together from across the country—and around the world—for a weekend filled with inspiration, learning, music, history, and connection.

While You're in Ohio

Visit the Cleveland Ohio Temple Open House

Since you'll already be in the area for Kirtland Heritage Days, we warmly encourage you to make time for this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity — a public tour of the newly completed Cleveland Ohio Temple before its dedication.

Event Details

Dates

Thursday, June 18 – Friday, July 3, 2026

Open house runs right alongside Heritage Days weekend!

Saturday: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Shorter wait times Monday–Thursday.

Address

5997 Brecksville Rd
Independence, OH 44131

Get Directions

Admission

Completely free and open to everyone — all ages, all backgrounds welcome.

The Tour

A guided walking tour lasting approximately 25 minutes. Wheelchair accessible. Business casual dress and comfortable shoes recommended.

A Unique, Once-in-a-Lifetime Opportunity

Once a temple is dedicated, it is no longer open to the general public. The Cleveland Ohio Temple Open House is a rare chance for members, friends, family, and neighbors to walk through this sacred space together before its dedication.

Since Heritage Days begins June 19 and the open house starts June 18, consider arriving a day early to tour the temple — or extend your stay into the following week to make a full Ohio faith heritage trip of it!

Good to Know

  • Carpooling is highly encouraged — on-site parking is limited
  • Shuttle service available from off-site parking
  • No photos or videos inside the temple
  • Leave large bags in your vehicle
