Escape to Serenity: Your Private Waite Hill Oasis Awaits!

Welcome to Old Orchard – your private Airbnb guesthouse retreat nestled in the serene beauty of Waite Hill! Discover a charming and unique escape where comfort meets tranquility. Our 2-story in-law suite offers a private entrance and even an elevator for your convenience.

Step outside and explore our expansive 8-acre property, featuring a full-court basketball court and a picturesque pond with enchanting waterfalls. Relax on our inviting patios, wander through vibrant flower beds, or gather around the huge fire pit for an evening under the stars.

Perfectly located, we're just a short walk from the historic Kirtland Temple and a scenic drive to Cuyahoga Valley National Park.
